Overview

The goal of this challenge was to reconstruct a meaningful sequence of actions from system evidence and identify the key event that answered the flag question.

Initial observation

I started by identifying what artifacts were available and what kind of timeline the challenge seemed to require.

Approach

  1. Review the prompt and identify the type of evidence likely to matter.
  2. Inspect the event logs and isolate the most relevant sources.
  3. Build a chronological view of meaningful events.
  4. Cross-check timestamps before concluding anything.
  5. Extract the final answer only after the timeline made sense.

Takeaway

The main lesson from this kind of challenge is that forensic analysis becomes much easier once the evidence is arranged as a timeline rather than treated as isolated entries.
