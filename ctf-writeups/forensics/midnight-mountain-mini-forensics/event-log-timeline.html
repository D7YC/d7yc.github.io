Overview

Initial observation

I started by identifying what artifacts were available and what kind of timeline the challenge seemed to require. Since the name and prompt suggested event-log work, the first step was to review the Windows log sources and look for user actions, process creation, or system events that stood out.

Approach

  1. Review the prompt and identify the type of evidence likely to matter.
  2. Inspect the event logs and isolate the most relevant log channels.
  3. Build a chronological view of suspicious or meaningful events.
  4. Cross-check timestamps to avoid drawing conclusions from a single entry.
  5. Extract the final answer only after the full timeline made sense.

Notes on reasoning
