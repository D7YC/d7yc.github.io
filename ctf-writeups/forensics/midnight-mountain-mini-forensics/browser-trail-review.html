Browser Trail Review

This second sample page is here to show how multiple challenges can live under one competition and still keep the archive tidy.

Track: Forensics Focus: Browser artifacts Status: Sample template

Overview

Browser-based forensic challenges often rely on a mix of history, downloads, cached content, cookies, or other small traces that become meaningful when combined.

What to document in a real writeup

  • What browser artifacts were provided or discovered
  • How the important clue was identified
  • Why certain artifacts mattered more than others
  • What the final answer depended on

Suggested structure

  1. Summarize the prompt and the likely evidence type.
  2. List the browser paths, files, or exported data you checked.
  3. Describe the clue chain clearly and in order.
  4. End with the answer and the lesson learned.

Why this page exists

Right now this page mainly acts as a styled placeholder. Once you publish a real challenge, replace this text with the actual walkthrough and keep the surrounding structure intact.
